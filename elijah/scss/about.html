Body Building

Bodybuilding is the practice of using physical exercise to develop and enhance the muscles of the body. This activity primarily involves strength training, weightlifting, and a high-protein diet to increase muscle mass and reduce body fat.

Weight Lefting

Weightlifting, often referred to as Olympic lifting, is a sport and form of exercise that focuses on lifting weights in the form of barbells. Unlike bodybuilding, which emphasizes muscle size and aesthetics, weightlifting concentrates on strength, power, and technique.

Muscle Building

Muscle building, also known as muscle hypertrophy, is the process of increasing the size and strength of muscles through resistance training and proper nutrition. The primary goal of muscle building is to create a well-defined, strong, and balanced physique.
